FAQ

Everything you need to know about ordering kulfi from Kulfi Kueen.

What is kulfi?

Kulfi is a traditional South Asian frozen dessert — denser and creamier than ice cream. It's slow-cooked and slow-frozen, giving it a rich, velvety texture that melts slowly on your tongue.

How do I place an order?

Use our online order form or call 610-428-3887. Tell us your flavors, quantities, and preferred pickup date. Mary will confirm availability and send pickup details.

Is delivery available?

Currently we offer pickup only in Orange County, CA. Pickup location and time are confirmed after your order is placed.

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering at least 2–3 days ahead since every batch is made fresh in small quantities. For catering or large orders, please allow 1–2 weeks.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Venmo (@KulfiKueen) and Zelle (orders@kulfikueen.com). Payment details are sent after Mary confirms your order.

Why aren't prices listed on the website?

Prices may vary by flavor, quantity, and season. Mary will provide a total when she confirms your order — no surprises, just great kulfi.

Do you have seasonal flavors?

Yes! We rotate limited-time flavors throughout the year — like Gulab Jamun in summer and Cranberry Pistachio in winter. Check our menu and blog for current specials.

Can you accommodate allergies or dietary needs?

Please note any allergies in your order form. Our kulfi contains dairy and nuts in many flavors. We're happy to discuss options — reach out via contact.

Do you offer catering or wholesale?

Absolutely! We cater weddings, parties, corporate events, and more. Visit our catering page or contact us for a custom quote.

How should I store kulfi?

Keep kulfi frozen until you're ready to enjoy. For best taste, let it sit at room temperature for 2–3 minutes before eating. Consume within 2 weeks of pickup.
