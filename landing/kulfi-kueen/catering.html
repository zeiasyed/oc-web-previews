How It Works

Simple Catering Process

  1. Reach out — Use our contact form or call 610-428-3887 with your event date, guest count, and flavor preferences.
  2. Custom quote — Mary will recommend flavors and quantities, and send a total.
  3. Confirm & pay — Secure your date with Venmo or Zelle payment.
  4. Pickup ready — Your kulfi is handcrafted fresh and ready for pickup before your event.

For large events (50+ guests), please book at least 2 weeks in advance.
