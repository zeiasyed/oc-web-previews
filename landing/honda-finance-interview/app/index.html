Before you decide

Know the difference

Both options get you into a Honda — but work differently. Read the basics, then answer a few questions.

Lease

Pay to use the car for a set term

  • Lower monthly payment
  • Return or buy at lease-end
  • Mileage limits (~10K mi/yr typical)

Finance (Buy)

Loan to own the vehicle

  • Higher payment, but you build equity
  • No mileage limits
  • Keep it after the loan is paid off

At a glance
